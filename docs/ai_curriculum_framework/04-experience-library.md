# PART FOUR: EXPERIENCE LIBRARY

## The Shift-Enabling Experiences

Each experience is designed to create conditions for a specific shift. Not to teach—to transform.

---

### Zone 0 → 1 Experiences

**The Hook**
- Show AI doing something surprising
- Zero explanation
- Let them feel "wow"
- Duration: 15 minutes

**The Mirror**
- Show AI doing something THEY do
- Their profession, their tasks
- "This is relevant to MY life"
- Duration: 20 minutes

**The Playground**
- Hand them the keyboard
- Zero pressure, pure exploration
- Facilitator stays quiet
- Duration: 30+ minutes

---

### Zone 1 → 2 Experiences

**Low-Stakes Wins**
- Tasks where being wrong doesn't matter
- Brainstorming, first drafts, summaries
- Accumulate positive experiences
- Duration: Days 1-3

**Medium-Stakes Application**
- Tasks that matter with safety nets
- They review/verify everything
- Build trust through success
- Duration: Days 4-7

**Real Integration**
- AI as normal workflow
- Reach for it automatically
- 3-5 established use cases
- Duration: Days 8-14

---

### Zone 2 → 3 Experiences

**The Contrast**
- Same task, two prompts (vague vs. rich)
- See the difference
- "Context IS the interface"
- Duration: 30 minutes

**The Explanation Practice**
- Write down context before prompting
- Audience, purpose, constraints
- "Like briefing a smart colleague"
- Duration: Per task

**The Multi-Turn Discovery**
- Complex task, 5+ turn conversation
- Track how output improves
- "Conversation IS the skill"
- Duration: 1 hour

**The Thinking Partner**
- Use AI for thinking, not producing
- "Help me think through..."
- "What am I missing?"
- Duration: Ongoing practice

---

### Zone 3 → 4 Experiences

**First Draft vs. Final Draft**
- Take "good enough" output
- Iterate for 20 minutes
- Compare before/after
- "First output is raw material"
- Duration: 30 minutes

**The Variation Game**
- Ask for 5 versions
- Formal, casual, bold, subtle
- Discover your preferences
- Duration: 20 minutes

**The Direction Techniques**
- Role assignment
- Example method
- Constraint method
- Critique method
- Duration: Practice each

**The Proud Creation**
- Create one thing you're genuinely proud of
- Iterate until excellent
- Quality that matters to you
- Duration: 1-2 hours

---

### Zone 4 → 5 Experiences

**The First Build**
- Custom GPT for something you need
- Identify need → Design → Build → Test → Share
- "I just built something"
- Duration: 2-3 hours

**Vibe Coding (5A)**
- Describe what you want
- AI generates code
- Test and iterate
- "Describing IS programming"
- Duration: Varies

**The Automation (5B)**
- Trigger → AI → Action
- Something with AI in the middle
- See it work automatically
- Duration: 2-4 hours

**The Deep Analysis (5C)**
- Complex problem with AI partnership
- What would take weeks manually
- Map variables, scenarios, implications
- Duration: Varies

---

### Zone 5 → 6 Experiences

**Reverse Engineering**
- Study AI-native products
- What couldn't exist without AI?
- How do components interact?
- Duration: 2-3 hours per product

**The Limit**
- Scale something you built 100x
- What breaks?
- Where does feature-thinking fail?
- Duration: 2 hours

**Design on Paper**
- Architect before building
- Multiple AI components
- Clear data flow
- Duration: Several sessions

**Build the System**
- Execute the architecture
- Learn from gaps
- Document decisions
- Duration: Several weeks

---

### Zone 6 → 7 Experiences

**The Question**
- "Should I have built this?"
- After any build
- Sit with discomfort
- Duration: Ongoing

**The Implications**
- Engage seriously with meaning
- Reading, dialogue, reflection
- No easy answers
- Duration: Ongoing

**Teaching**
- Teach AI to someone
- Face questions you can't answer
- Gaps reveal what's unintegrated
- Duration: Ongoing
