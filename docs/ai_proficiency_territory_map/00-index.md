# THE AI PROFICIENCY TERRITORY MAP
## A Cartographer's Guide to Perception Shifts in the Age of AI

*Not a skills ladder. A map of how humans see—and could see—artificial intelligence.*

---

**Core Premise:**
AI proficiency is not about what you *know* or what you can *do*.
It's about how you *perceive* AI and your relationship to it.

Skills follow perception. Never the reverse.
