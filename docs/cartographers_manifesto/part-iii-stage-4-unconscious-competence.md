### Stage 4: UNCONSCIOUS COMPETENCE
**"I just do it. I can't explain how."**

**Characteristics:**
- Flow state is normal
- Intuition guides decisions
- Performance looks magical to others
- "It just feels right"

**What they see:**
The invisible forces directly (but cannot articulate them)

**What they don't see:**
Why they see what they see (meta-awareness missing)

**The problem:**
Cannot teach it. Cannot explain it. Cannot scale it.
Makes decisions that work, but doesn't know why.
