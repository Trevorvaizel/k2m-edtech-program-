### Tool 6: CONSTRAINT EXPLORATION
**Master by Limiting Yourself**

**The Process:**
1. Remove your favorite tools/techniques
2. Force yourself to achieve results with what remains
3. Notice what becomes impossible
4. Notice what still works
5. The things that still work = the principles

**The Result:**
You discover the leverage points (Force 1).
You stop relying on crutches.
You understand what actually matters.
