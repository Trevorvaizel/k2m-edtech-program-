### Stage 0: UNCONSCIOUS INCOMPETENCE
**"I don't know what I don't know"**

**Characteristics:**
- Confident but wrong
- Oversimplifies everything
- "This can't be that hard"
- Ignores details
- Thinks talent matters more than work

**What they see:**
The visible layer only (the performance, the artifact)

**What they don't see:**
The invisible forces (all of Part II)

**The danger:**
Dunning-Kruger effect - peak confidence at minimum competence
