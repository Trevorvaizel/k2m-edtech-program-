### Stage 3: CONSCIOUS COMPETENCE (FLUID)
**"I can do it without thinking about the basics"**

**Characteristics:**
- Techniques are automatic
- Can focus on higher-level problems
- Performance is consistent
- "I'm good at this" (dangerous confidence returns)

**What they see:**
The dynamics layer - how things interact

**What they don't see:**
The invisible forces driving the dynamics

**The trap:**
Second Dunning-Kruger peak - "I've mastered this"
No, you've mastered the mechanics and dynamics.
The invisible forces still rule you.
