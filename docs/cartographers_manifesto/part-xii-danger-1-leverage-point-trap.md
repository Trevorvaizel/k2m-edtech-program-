### Danger 1: THE LEVERAGE POINT TRAP
**Optimization Without Ethics**

**The Problem:**
- Asymmetric leverage (80/20) is morally neutral
- You can optimize for good OR evil
- "Efficient genocide" is still leverage (applied horribly)

**The Example:**
- Facebook's engagement algorithm: Optimized for attention (leverage)
- Result: Destroyed democracy, mental health, society (unintended consequences)
- The force worked. The purpose was wrong.

**The Antidote:**
Ask: "What SHOULD I optimize for?" before asking "What creates 80% of results?"

**Principle:**
Leverage amplifies purpose. If your purpose is wrong, leverage makes it MORE wrong.
