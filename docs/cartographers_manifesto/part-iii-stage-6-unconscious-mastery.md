### Stage 6: UNCONSCIOUS MASTERY
**"I embody the territory. I am the map."**

**Characteristics:**
- No separation between self and skill
- Decisions happen before conscious thought
- Innovations emerge without effort
- Presence creates transformation in others

**What they see:**
Reality itself, not just the field

**What they create:**
New paradigms, new fields, new possibilities

**Examples (field-agnostic):**
- The musician who redefines what music is
- The chef who transforms how people think about food
- The surgeon who invents entirely new approaches
- The teacher who changes how education works

**This is transcendence.**
