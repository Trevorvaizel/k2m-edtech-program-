### Stage 2: CONSCIOUS COMPETENCE (MECHANICAL)
**"I can do it if I focus hard"**

**Characteristics:**
- Can perform techniques in isolation
- Needs to think through every step
- Slow, deliberate, exhausting
- "I'm getting it" (but not really)

**What they see:**
Individual mechanics working

**What they don't see:**
How mechanics interact (emergence)

**The trap:**
Plateau - can do the work, but cannot improve further
This is where **most professionals stay forever**
