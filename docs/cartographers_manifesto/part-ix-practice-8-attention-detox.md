### Practice 8: THE ATTENTION DETOX
**Monthly Practice: Reclaim Your Focus**

**The Problem:**
Modern attention is fragmented by:
- Notifications
- Multitasking
- Infinite scrolling
- Context switching
- Reactive work

**The Practice:**
**Once per month, spend one day with:**
- Phone off
- Single-tasking only
- No social media
- Deep work on ONE thing
- No distractions

**The Result:**
- Resets attention span
- Reveals how fragmented normal attention is
- Practices Layer 4 focus (strategic allocation)
