### Stage 5: CONSCIOUS MASTERY
**"I can do it AND explain why it works"**

**Characteristics:**
- Can access unconscious competence consciously
- Can break down "intuition" into teachable principles
- Can innovate and create new approaches
- Performance is intentional, not accidental

**What they see:**
The full territory - visible, mechanics, dynamics, invisible

**What they can do:**
- Teach Stage 0-3 people effectively
- Diagnose problems others cannot see
- Predict failures before they happen
- Shift contexts deliberately

**This is true expertise.**
