### Stage 1: CONSCIOUS INCOMPETENCE
**"I now know how much I don't know"**

**Characteristics:**
- Humility crashes in
- Overwhelmed by complexity
- "This is impossible"
- Sees gaps everywhere
- Wants to quit (90% do here)

**What they see:**
The mechanics layer - thousands of techniques to learn

**What they don't see:**
The patterns that connect techniques

**The opportunity:**
This is where **real learning begins** - but it feels like failure
