## 🌉 **MOCKUP 1: Celestial Bridge Convergence**

**File:** `k2m-landing/mockup-1-celestial-bridge.html`

**Concept:**
- Three orbital paths rotate around a central hub
- Each path represents a bridge to a different future
- Scroll to activate each orbital bridge
- Click to "cross the bridge" and see full details

**GSAP Features:**
- ✅ Continuous orbital rotation (60s loop)
- ✅ Scroll-based bridge activation (3 scroll zones)
- ✅ Scale and glow transitions
- ✅ Modal reveal with elastic easing

**Interaction:**
1. Scroll down → Bridges rotate and pulse
2. Each scroll position activates a different bridge
3. Click bridge label → Opens modal with full details
4. Modal has "Cross the Bridge" CTA

**Best For:**
- Analytical decision-makers
- Users who want to explore all options
- Space/cosmic theme lovers

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-High)

## 🪞 **MOCKUP 2: Mirror Hall with Velvet Rope**

**File:** `k2m-landing/mockup-2-mirror-hall.html`

**Concept:**
- Three ornate mirrors with veiled surfaces
- Hover to lift the veil and see your future self
- Each mirror reflects a different version of you
- Exclusive, VIP feel

**GSAP Features:**
- ✅ Staggered entrance animation
- ✅ Hover-triggered veil lift (0.8s cubic easing)

- ✅ Future self reveal with scale transform

**Interaction:**
1. Scroll down → Mirrors float into view
2. Hover mirror → Veil lifts, future self appears
3. Future self shows: "Future You: The Pioneer/Educator/Architect"
4. Click CTA → "Step Into Your Future"

**Best For:**
- Brand-conscious users
- Self-discovery focused individuals
- Premium/exclusive positioning

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ (Medium)

## 🚪 **MOCKUP 5: Progressive Door Stack**

**File:** `k2m-landing/mockup-5-door-stack.html`

**Concept:**
- Three doors stand before you
- Each has sounds and light spilling from beneath
- Swipe/peek to see scenes behind doors
- Step through into the experience

**GSAP Features:**
- ✅ Card stack physics (drag resistance, spring tension)
- ✅ Parallax peek effect (20% next card visible)
- ✅ Door swing open animation
- ✅ Scene-in-motion reveal

**Interaction:**
1. See 3 doors with ambient light/sound
2. Swipe/drag to peek behind doors
3. Each door shows: people learning, building, teaching
4. Select → Door swings wide, scene comes alive
5. Step through → Transition to program page

**Best For:**
- Action-oriented users
- Mobile-first experience
- Tactile/physical interaction lovers

**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ (Medium - familiar pattern)
