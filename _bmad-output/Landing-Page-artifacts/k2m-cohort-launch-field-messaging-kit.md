## 8) Objection One-Liners (Fast Replies)

- `What is this exactly?`
  8-week readiness cohort for Form 4 leavers. 4 habits, one proof artifact, pre-campus.

- `Is this just AI classes?`
  No. It is a thinking program. AI is a tool in the process, not the outcome.

- `My child already uses ChatGPT.`
  Good start. K2M focuses on structured thinking and navigation, not random tool use.

- `Will this help at university?`
  Yes. The outcome is readiness for unfamiliar subjects from Week 1, with visible proof.

- `How much is it?`
  Full fee `[FULL_FEE_KES]`. Reserve seat with `[DEPOSIT_KES]` today.

## 9) 7-Day Action Sprint (Concrete)

### Day 1
- Publish 2 student status posts + 1 parent status post.
- Send 5 connector first messages.
- Do 1 school walk-in.

### Day 2
- Follow up 10 inbound DMs to Rung 3.
- Close deposits for all YES replies.
- Send 2 parent proof messages.

### Day 3
- Publish 2 fresh status posts.
- Send 5 new connector messages.
- Book 1 school demo slot.

### Day 4
- Run orientation call / info session.
- Move all hot leads to commitment (deposit ask).

### Day 5
- Publish scarcity update using real seat count.
- Close remaining warm leads.

### Day 6
- School follow-up + parent voice-note distribution.
- Deposit closeout push.

### Day 7
- Final 24h close sequence.
- Confirm enrolled list and orientation.
