## THE WALK-IN SCRIPT

### THE OPENER — AT THE GATE OR RECEPTION
*(30 seconds. Calm. Not rushed.)*

> "Good morning. My name is Trevor — I'm the program director for K2M, a pre-university readiness cohort we're running in partnership with Le Wagon — they're a French coding institution with campuses in 44 cities, including Paris, London, and across East Africa."

*Brief pause. Let Le Wagon land.*

> "We're selecting 5 pioneer schools in Nairobi for our first sponsored cohort. We're not approaching every school — we're looking for institutions whose Form 4 leavers are ready to be part of something that will follow them all the way to university and beyond. I'd love 10 minutes with you or your head of Form 4."

---

### ACT 1 — IN THE ROOM WITH THE PRINCIPAL OR TEACHER
*(Once seated — 2 minutes)*

> "Here is what we're doing and why we came to your school specifically."

> "K2M is an 8-week cohort for Form 4 leavers — the gap between school and university. Most students waste that window. Our program gives them 4 structured thinking habits that let them walk into any university subject they've never studied and actually navigate it. Not survive — navigate."

> "We're running this first cohort in association with Le Wagon France. Their students have gone on to careers at Google, HSBC, Airbnb — and in Kenya, this is the first time a program like this is being offered at the pre-university level."

*Then — the pivot that makes them sit forward:*

> "We're not selling seats. K2M is sponsoring 75% of the program cost for every pioneer student. Your job is simply to nominate them."

---

### ACT 2 — THE PIONEER OFFER
*(The heart of the script — 60 seconds)*

> "Here's the structure of the partnership:"

> "We select 5 schools. Each school nominates 20 of their best Form 4 leavers — your strongest finishers, the students you'd stake your reputation on. K2M covers 75% of the program cost for each of those 20 students. The pioneer contribution from each student is [PIONEER_FEE] KES — against a full program value of [FULL_FEE] KES."

> "Why are we doing this? Because we need a pioneer cohort — real students, real results — to prove this program works in the Kenyan market. Your school gets to say your leavers were part of the founding class of an internationally-partnered program. Those students get a Pioneer designation in their profile that travels with them to campus."

*Then — name the pain you discovered:*

> "One of the teachers I spoke to recently mentioned something that stuck with me — the CBC assessments that come in randomly from the county, the marking schemes, the schedule of works — the amount of time that takes. I want to show you something before I leave. Two minutes."

---

### ACT 3 — THE LIVE PROOF
*(The trust-builder — 2-3 minutes)*

> "This is what your teachers are dealing with."

*Pull out phone or laptop. Ask for the last CBC topic or any topic they mention. Live demo:*
- Draft a marking scheme in under 3 minutes
- Structure a Schedule of Works for a unit
- Generate tiered assessment questions at different difficulty levels

> "This is the kind of structured thinking we teach your students — not to use AI as a shortcut, but to direct it like an expert. The teacher who knows how to use this saves 5 hours a week. The student who learns this before campus can walk into any subject they've never formally studied."

> "That's what your 20 pioneers learn to do."

---

### ACT 4 — THE CLOSE
*(One ask. One door.)*

> "We need one thing from your school: a nomination list. Your top 20 Form 4 leavers. Teacher-identified, merit-based — you know your students better than anyone."

> "We have two orientation dates for pioneer schools: [DATE + TIME] and [DATE + TIME]. Which one would work for your school to send their nominees?"

*Stop. Let them answer.*

---

### HANDLING THE KEY MOMENTS

**If they ask: "What is Le Wagon exactly?"**
> "Le Wagon is a French coding bootcamp — 44 campuses globally, including London, Paris, Singapore. They partner with universities like HEC Paris and Imperial College. We're running this cohort in association with them as the foundational thinking layer before students go into technical training. K2M is the thinking system. Le Wagon is the technical pathway after university."

**If they ask: "What does 75% sponsored mean — who pays what?"**
> "K2M covers 75% of the full program cost for every pioneer student. The student's contribution is [PIONEER_FEE] KES — against a program that is worth [FULL_FEE] KES. We do this for the pioneer cohort because we need real students and real results. After this intake, the standard rate applies."

**If they ask: "What do we get as a school?"**
> "Your school is listed as a K2M Pioneer Partner School. Your leavers carry a pioneer cohort designation. After Week 8, each student produces a proof artifact — real work that shows they've navigated a domain they'd never studied. That becomes part of your school's leaver outcomes story."

**If they say "I need to think about it":**
> "Completely understand. We're finalising the 5 schools this week — if your school isn't confirmed, we move to the next on our list. I can hold your slot until [DATE] — would that give you enough time to decide?"

**If they ask: "Is this only for strong students?"**
> "We ask the school to nominate their best 20 — because the pioneer cohort needs students who will engage seriously and produce results. Those results are what we use to demonstrate the program to future schools and future cohorts."

## WHY THE 75% SPONSORSHIP SELLS

```
"FREE"              →  Feels like low value.
                        "If it were good, why is it free?"

"DISCOUNTED"        →  Feels transactional.
                        Signals desperation.

"75% SPONSORED"     →  Feels like selection.
                        "K2M is investing in these students.
                         They chose us."

"PIONEER FEE"       →  The 25% the student pays is not
                        a remainder. It is a commitment.
                        Skin in the game.
                        The program works better when
                        students have invested something.

FULL VALUE ANCHOR   →  [FULL_FEE] KES is shown first.
                        [PIONEER_FEE] KES lands as contrast.
                        The value is felt before the
                        number is revealed.
                        This is anchoring — not discounting.
```
