## 💬 Copywriting Techniques Used

### 1. The "You're Not Alone" Opening
**Why:** Instantly validates user's hidden feeling
**Effect:** Reduces shame, builds trust

### 2. Problem-Agitation-Solution (PAS)
**Problem:** "AI works sometimes, confuses other times"
**Agitation:** "Here's what nobody tells you"
**Solution:** "It's that nobody taught you how to think"
**Effect:** Creates relief through reframe

### 3. The Metaphor (Territory Map)
**Zone 0-4 as geographic journey**
**Why:** Makes abstract concrete
**Effect:** "I can see my path"

### 4. Social Proof as Theater
**Chat bubbles, not testimonials**
**Why:** Shows community is alive
**Effect:** "I want to be part of this"

### 5. The Diagnostic as Gift
**"Not a test. No right answers."**
**Why:** Removes pressure
**Effect:** "This is safe"

### 6. The No-Obligation Close
**"Then decide if the cohort is right for you"**
**Why:** Reverses risk
**Effect:** Builds trust

## 🔤 Typography & Formatting Guidelines

### Font Hierarchy:
```
H1 (Headlines): Space Grotesk 700
H2 (Subheadlines): Space Grotesk 600
Body: Inter 400
Emphasis: Inter 600 (semibold)
Ocean Mint: Custom glow effect
```

### Line Length:
- **Desktop:** 60-75 characters per line (optimal readability)
- **Mobile:** 40-55 characters per line
- **Rationale:** Prevents eye fatigue, maintains flow

### Line Height:
- **Body text:** 1.6 (160% of font size)
- **Headlines:** 1.2 (tighter for impact)
- **Rationale:** Breathability without looseness

### Paragraph Spacing:
- **Between paragraphs:** 1.5em (generous white space)
- **Rationale:** "Relief needs room to breathe"
