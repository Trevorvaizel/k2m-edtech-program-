## TYPOGRAPHY

### Headlines
- **Font:** Montserrat ExtraBold or Impact (Google Fonts)
- **Color:** Sunrise Orange (#FF6B35)
- **Style:** ALL CAPS, bold, tight tracking

### Body Text
- **Font:** Inter or Open Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Color:** White on Navy, Navy on light
- **Size:** 14-18pt depending on format
- **Style:** Regular weight, readable

### Call-to-Actions
- **Font:** Montserrat Bold or SemiBold
- **Color:** White text on Orange background
- **Style:** Centered, prominent, button-style

## IMAGERY GUIDELINES

### DO:
- Use action shots (boxing in motion, AI demo engagement, talent performances)
- Show diverse youth (male, female, different ages)
- Include upward movement visual metaphors (ladders, arrows, rising sun)
- Use high-quality photography (not clip art)
- Show people DOING, not posing

### DON'T:
- No faces (political protection - avoid personality cult)
- No stock photos of "poor African children" (dignity first)
- No politician imagery (this is anti-political branding)
- No cheesy graphics or clip art (professional aesthetic)

### Image Sourcing:
- **If using real photos:** Take them at grassroots events (boxing, modeling programs)
- **If using stock:** Use authentic-looking African youth in action
- **If no images:** Go typography-only (Option 3) - still powerful

## DESIGN TIPS

### Hierarchy
1. **Most important:** Event name + date (people need to know WHAT and WHEN)
2. **Second:** Ticket price + CTA (how to attend)
3. **Third:** What's happening (boxing, AI, talent, networking)
4. **Fourth:** Narrative (why this matters)

### Visual Flow
- **Top:** Logo/Event name (grab attention)
- **Middle:** What + When + Where (details)
- **Bottom:** CTA (what to do next)

### Contrast
- Navy background = White text
- Orange accents = Draw eye to key info
- Gold elements = Highlight premium features (sponsors, VIP)

### Politics Protection
- No faces (anti-personality cult)
- No "MP endorsed" (anti-political)
- No "sponsored by [politician]" (anti-patronage)
- Clean, professional, movement-based

**Political Neutrality By Design:**

We partner with grassroots TALENT PIPELINES, not political FACTIONS.

- Boxing clubs = athletes, not ward politics
- Modeling programs = aspiring models, not clan networks
- Talent showcases = performers, not voting blocs

**Talent has no party. Skills have no tribe.**

## BRAND GUIDELINES

### Logo Usage
- Use "KASIPUL RISE" logo (provided separately)
- Minimum size: 100px width for digital
- Clear space: 50% around logo

### Color Usage
- **Primary:** Navy (#0A2342) for backgrounds
- **Accent:** Orange (#FF6B35) for CTAs, highlights
- **Premium:** Gold (#D4AF37) for sponsors, VIP
- **Neutral:** White (#FFFFFF) for text on dark

### Tone
- **Bold, not aggressive** (confidence, not threat)
- **Professional, not corporate** (authentic, not sterile)
- **Movement-oriented, not organizational** (energy, not bureaucracy)
