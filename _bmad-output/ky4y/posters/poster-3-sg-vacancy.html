Call for Leadership  |  18th February 2026
★   Kasipul, We Need You   ★
Your Community
Is Calling You to Serve.
We are looking for two young people from Kasipul — people who are already out there, already doing something, already giving back — to carry this organisation forward.
Two Open Positions
Position One
Secretary General
The lead role. You speak for our members, keep things moving, and make sure our work in the community is felt.
Position Two
Assistant Secretary General
You work alongside the SG, cover what they cannot, and make sure no member and no ward is left behind.
ⓘ   One position for a male candidate, one for a female candidate — both must be filled.
Who We Are Looking For
✦
An active Kasipul youth — You must be a member currently involved in grassroot development in this constituency. This is not for observers. It is for those already in the field.
✦
Genuinely serving — Not serving on paper. Not serving for position. We are looking for someone whose community can already see their impact.
✦
Dedicated to youth — Your motivation must be the youth of Kasipul — their growth, their dignity, their future. Everything else is secondary.
Application Deadline
Wednesday, 25th February 2026
Late applications will not be considered. Submit before the deadline.
How to Apply
1
Write a Letter Tell us who you are and why you want to serve.
2
State Your Impact Tell us what you have already done in this community.
3
Send It In Email or deliver to the Chairman by the deadline.
