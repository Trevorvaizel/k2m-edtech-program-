But before I talk about what it could mean for students, I want to show you something that could be valuable to YOU personally. Would that be okay?

*[Wait for confirmation]*

---

**KEY POINTS:**
- Reference his actual speech (shows you did homework)
- Connect to HIS language: dignity, Gen Z, healing
- Frame: dignity → capability → agency
- Shift from abstract to concrete: "show you something"
- Ask permission (creates buy-in)

## VALIDATION FRAMEWORK

### Success Indicators (What You're Looking For)

**DURING MEETING:**
- ✅ Boyd leans forward during demo (engagement)
- ✅ Boyd asks "How did it know that?" or similar (believability)
- ✅ Boyd shares a REAL problem unprompted (trust)
- ✅ Boyd tries the advisory council himself during meeting (ownership)
- ✅ Boyd commits to trying it for 1-2 weeks (commitment)
- ✅ Boyd mentions specific schools/parents/timing (concrete thinking)

**WEEK 1 POST-MEETING:**
- ✅ Boyd actually uses the advisory council (validates personal value)
- ✅ Boyd mentions it to colleagues/staff (viral signal)
- ✅ Boyd asks follow-up questions about the student program (sustained interest)
- ✅ Boyd responds to your check-in within 48 hours (priority signal)

**WEEK 2-3 (PARENT PRESENTATION SETUP):**
- ✅ Boyd commits to specific date/school for parent session (action)
- ✅ Boyd offers to introduce you with personal testimonial (champion behavior)
- ✅ Boyd helps promote the session to parents (co-ownership)

### Risk Flags (What to Watch Out For)

**RED FLAGS:**
- 🚩 Boyd doesn't respond to any follow-ups (not interested)
- 🚩 Boyd says "yes" but never commits to specific dates (polite brush-off)
- 🚩 Boyd delegates you to someone else without endorsement (passing the buck)
- 🚩 Boyd asks for payment/funding before pilot (misaligned incentives)

**YELLOW FLAGS:**
- ⚠️ Boyd seems interested but "needs to check with others" (bureaucratic blockers)
- ⚠️ Boyd wants to see it work elsewhere first (not willing to be first)
- ⚠️ Boyd focuses only on personal advisory council, not student program (missing the point)

### Decision Points

**IF Boyd validates value but wants to start smaller:**
- Offer: "What if we do a single workshop first? 3-hour session with 20-30 students, you observe, we measure the shift. If it works, we scale to full pilot."

**SOLUTION:** Have backup screenshots, pivot to conceptual
