## Canonical Behavior (Implemented)

1. Weekly parent updates run every **Monday at 9:00 AM EAT** and summarize the **previous cohort week**.
2. Week 8 parent reports run every **Friday at 9:00 AM EAT** in Week 8.
3. Weekly updates are sent only for students with `consent_preference = share_weekly` and `parent_opted_out = 0`.
4. Week 8 reports include both `share_weekly` and `privacy_first` records, but skip students who opted out.
5. Week 8 reports are sent only for published artifacts; artifacts with `visibility_level = private` are skipped and logged.
6. Every email includes an unsubscribe link; unsubscribe is a hard opt-out from future parent emails.
7. Weekly update sends are idempotent per student/report-week (prevents duplicate sends after restarts).
