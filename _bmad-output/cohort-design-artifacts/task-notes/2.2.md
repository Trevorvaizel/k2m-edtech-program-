**Participation Tracking Features:**
- Records first post time, post count, engagement score
- Flags inactive students at 6 PM (flagged_inactive = 1)
- Tracks whether nudge was sent (nudge_sent = 1)
- Week-specific nudge messages (e.g., Week 1: "No pressure", Week 4: "New tools unlocked")
- Private to dashboard (never posted publicly)
- DM permissions handled gracefully (Forbidden exception)

**Evening Snapshot Features:**
- Anonymized aggregate patterns (no student names)
- Participation count and engagement average
- Sample posts (without identifying information)
- Guardrail #3 compliance message ("No comparison, no ranking")
- Wednesday peer visibility moment support

**Bot Reaction System:**
- Reaction queue for batch processing (up to 10 at once)
- 👀 emoji for all posts (witnessed)
- Async error handling (NotFound, Forbidden)
- Processes queued reactions every 10 new messages

**Engagement Scoring (1-6 scale):**
1: Very brief (< 20 chars)
2: Brief but complete (20-50 chars)
3: Standard response (50-150 chars)
4: Thoughtful response (150-300 chars)
5: Detailed reflection (300+ chars, examples)
6: Exceptional (multi-paragraph, deep thinking, authentic voice)

**Database Integration:**
- daily_participation table with FOREIGN KEY to students
- UNIQUE constraint on (discord_id, date) - one record per student per day
- Indexes for date, student, flagged_inactive, week_number
- Atomic updates (post_count++, engagement_score = MAX())
