## 3) Success Model (Second-Order + Business Outcomes)

## 3.1 Second-Order Achievements (Strategic)

K2M wins when audience perception shifts from:
- "AI tools training" -> "cognitive transformation"
- "surface use" -> "second-order thinking"
- "fragile skills" -> "compounding judgment"

## 3.2 Operational Success Layers

| Layer | What Success Looks Like | Leading Indicators | Lagging Indicators |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cognitive shift | Audience recognizes K2M as thinking-first, not tools-first | "I can't unsee it" comments, saved posts, thoughtful replies | Higher trust and easier conversion in later touches |
| Behavioral shift | People take low-friction commitments | Quiz starts/completions, downloads, replay/watch-through | More warm audiences entering conversion pool |
| Pipeline | Intent becomes action | Conversion CTR, applications started, pilot call bookings | Applications completed, qualified pipeline |
| Business | Sustainable growth with brand integrity | Weekly 70/30 compliance, altitude compliance, low churn signals | Cohort fill rate, principal partnership closes, repeat referrals |

## 3.3 Weekly Executive Scoreboard

Track weekly:
- Applications started
- Applications completed
- Quiz completion count
- Quiz -> application conversion rate
- Download -> application conversion rate
- Principal pilot calls booked
- 70/30 Give vs Ask compliance
- Altitude compliance rate (target: 100%)

## 4) Execution Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. North Star check first: no post ships without "thinking WITH AI" alignment.
2. Altitude check second: language must match persona JTBD altitude.
3. Temperature check third: cold traffic gets trust-building content, not hard asks.
4. 5-Gear check on conversion posts:
- Gear 1 Friction
- Gear 2 Entry
- Gear 3 Cognitive load/hook
- Gear 4 Visual hierarchy
- Gear 5 Timeline/urgency
5. Ethics and brand:
- Maintain 70/30 Give vs Ask across the month.
- Use inevitability and clarity, not manipulative fear.

## 8) Weekly Optimization Loop

Run every Friday:

1. Measure:
- By content type KPI
- By persona and temperature
- By touch stage drop-off

2. Diagnose:
- If starts high, completions low -> reduce form friction and add FAQ proof.
- If method engagement high, conversion low -> add stronger Thursday timeline urgency.
- If manifesto engagement high, method weak -> make Tuesday more concrete.
- If principal interest high, calls low -> tighten pilot proposal CTA and calendar friction.

3. Adjust next week:
- Keep winning hooks and CTAs.
- Replace underperforming opening lines.
- Rebalance audience targeting spend.
