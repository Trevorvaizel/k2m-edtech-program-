## 1. BACKGROUND

1.1 The Promoter is in the process of establishing a private company limited by shares in Kenya under the Companies Act, 2015.

1.2 The proposed company name has reached the name reservation stage with the Registrar of Companies.

1.3 The Company has not yet been incorporated.

1.4 The Investor desires to make a financial contribution in exchange for equity in the Company, subject to incorporation and completion of Cohort One.

---

## 2. PURPOSE

This Agreement sets out the terms under which the Investor shall provide funding in exchange for a contractual right to receive equity in the Company upon incorporation and fulfillment of the conditions set out herein.

---

## 3. INVESTMENT AMOUNT

3.1 The Investor shall pay Kenya Shillings Ten Thousand (KES 10,000) to the Promoter within ___ days of signing this Agreement.

3.2 The funds shall be transferred to the account designated by the Promoter.

---

## 4. EQUITY ENTITLEMENT

4.1 In consideration of the investment of KES 10,000, the Investor shall be entitled to three percent (3%) of the total issued share capital of the Company.

4.2 The 3% equity shall be calculated on a fully diluted basis at the time of incorporation.

4.3 The entitlement to shares shall only crystallize upon fulfillment of the following conditions:

   a. Formal incorporation of the Company under Kenyan law; and
   b. Completion of Cohort One as defined by the Promoter's operational framework.

4.4 The shares shall be formally issued and reflected in the Company Register of Members within thirty (30) days after both conditions are satisfied.

---

## 5. PRE INCORPORATION STATUS

5.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Company does not legally exist at the time of signing this Agreement.

5.2 This Agreement creates a contractual right to receive shares upon incorporation and does not constitute immediate issuance of shares.

5.3 Upon incorporation, the Promoter shall ensure that the Company adopts this Agreement and issues shares consistent with its terms.

---

## 6. USE OF FUNDS

The investment funds shall be used for:

- Operational costs related to Cohort One;
- Administrative and compliance expenses;
- Company registration and related statutory costs;
- Business development activities aligned with the Company vision.

---

## 7. RISK ACKNOWLEDGMENT

The Investor acknowledges that:

- The Company is at an early stage of development;
- There are inherent business risks;
- Returns are not guaranteed;
- The value of shares may increase or decrease;
- Future fundraising may dilute shareholding.

---

## 8. FAILURE TO INCORPORATE

8.1 If the Company is not incorporated within ___ months from the date of this Agreement, the Parties shall meet to determine an appropriate resolution.

8.2 The Promoter may, at his discretion, refund the investment amount without interest.

8.3 Refund of the investment shall terminate the Investor's right to equity under this Agreement.

---

## 9. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

9.1 Upon issuance of shares, the Investor shall not transfer, assign, or dispose of the shares without written consent of the Company, subject to any Shareholders Agreement to be executed after incorporation.

---

## 10. FUTURE SHAREHOLDERS AGREEMENT

10.1 The Parties agree that upon incorporation, a formal Shareholders Agreement may be executed to govern:

- Voting rights;
- Dividend policy;
- Pre emptive rights;
- Dilution provisions;
- Exit mechanisms;
- Governance structure.

---

## 11. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Investor agrees to maintain confidentiality regarding the Company's business plans, financial information, strategy, intellectual property, and operational frameworks.

---

## 12. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Kenya.

Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall first be resolved through amicable negotiations. Failing resolution, the dispute shall be referred to arbitration in Nairobi in accordance with the Arbitration Act of Kenya.

---

## 13. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties regarding the subject matter herein and supersedes any prior oral or written discussions.
